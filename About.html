About Us

Laser Clean is a project created by dedicated and passionate physicists looking to make the method of wind turbine cleaning a more efficient and safer process. With this project we believe we can help Ireland meet the EU Renewable Energy Directive and make the world a better place.

The core values of the team are:

Innovation • Efficiency • Transparency • Quality

Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated scientists who specialise in laser physics, focusing on the practical applications laser spectroscopy can have in the real world. Our lead principal investigator John T. Costello has over 38 years of experience using his knowledge of physics to provide companies with convenient and cost effective solutions to their problems.


Our passion for green energy and using what we have learned to make the world a cleaner and safer place to live drives our ambition to deliver a revolutionary method for wind turbine cleaning - It keeps us on the cutting edge of renewable energy in terms of published research and collaboration opportunities.

What We Do

Using our suite of optical laboratories equipped with expensive laser systems and CCD Camera arrays we can provide industries with quick and precise analysis of sample spectra for samples such as wind turbine offcut. These laser setups can determine the chemical makeup of any material right down to trace elements.


These techniques along with our ingenuity will result in a dual laser spectroscopy system built into a custom designed chassis which can be deployed at wind turbine sites around the country to effectively and cost efficiently clean wind turbines without the need for harsh chemicals and long down times.
